About Mini Side

Our Story


Mini Side was born out of a passion for storytelling and a deep appreciation for African culture and heritage. We believe that comics are a powerful medium to convey complex narratives and spark meaningful conversations. Our goal is to bring the rich tapestry of African mythology and contemporary issues to a global audience through our unique and engaging comics.
